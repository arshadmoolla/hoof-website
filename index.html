Welcome to HOOF!

Home  Of  Our  Friends

We are a non-profit animal rescue centre that provides a variety of high quality animal care services for free.

We also act as a temporary safe haven for homeless, abondoned and neglected animals until we are able tofind them better, more suitable homes.

Sign up for our newsletter and get notified about our latest events and initiatives!


SOME OF THE WONDERFUL WORK WE DO

Free & Quality Veterinary Care


High quality medical care for your pets can be very costly and many animals suffer without it. That is why we provide this service for free.

Shelter for Homeless Animals


Our 216 HOOF! centres spread accross the country serve as temporary shelters for animals who have been abondoned neglected and abused while our agents work tirelessly to rehome them.

Adoption and Rehoming


We strongly believe that all animals deserve should be loved and cared for. Therefore we spend a lot of time and effort trying to find suitable homes for our homeless animals.
